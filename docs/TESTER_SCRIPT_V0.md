# Tester Script V0

## Beta Hairstyle Try-On Demo

This is a beta hairstyle try-on demo.

You can use it to:
- take or upload a real photo
- get hairstyle recommendations
- try different hairstyles
- try different hair colors
- generate an AI preview
- save your favorite look
- view saved looks in Profile History

## Before Testing

For the best result:
- use a clear selfie
- make sure your full face is visible
- use good lighting
- avoid very dark photos
- avoid extreme side angles
- avoid heavy filters
- keep hair visible if possible

## Step-by-Step Tester Flow

### 1. Open the app
- Open the app on your phone.
- Wait for the main screen to load.

### 2. Open Try AI
- Tap `Try AI`.

### 3. Add your photo
- Tap either:
  - `Take Photo`, or
  - `Upload from Gallery`
- Choose a clear face photo.

### 4. Confirm your photo
- Review the photo preview.
- Tap `Confirm Photo`.

### 5. Analyze your look
- Tap `Analyze My Look`.
- Wait for the app to show hairstyle recommendations.

### 6. Review recommendations
- Look at the recommendation groups.
- Read the style suggestions.
- Pick one hairstyle you want to test.

### 7. Choose a hairstyle
- Tap `Try This Look` on a recommendation card.
- Make sure the app shows that style as selected.

### 8. Choose a hair color
- Pick a hair color from the available color options.

### 9. Generate the first preview
- Tap `Generate This Look`.
- Wait for the preview image to appear.

### 10. Review the preview
- Check whether the hairstyle looks useful.
- Check whether the preview still looks like the same person.
- Check whether the selected color looks correct.

### 11. Change color and generate again
- Choose a different hair color.
- Tap `Generate Updated Look`.
- Compare the new result.

### 12. Change hairstyle and generate again
- Choose another hairstyle from the recommendations.
- Tap `Try This Look`.
- Tap `Generate Updated Look`.
- Compare the new result.

### 13. Save your look
- When you find a result you like, tap `Save Look`.

### 14. Check saved history
- Open `Profile`.
- Confirm the saved look appears in `Profile History`.
- Confirm the saved history card shows the preview thumbnail if available.

### 15. Optional delete test
- If asked by the team, tap `Delete` on a saved history item.
- Confirm the item is removed.

## What Feedback to Give

Please share feedback in simple language:
- Was the app easy to understand?
- Was it easy to know what to do next?
- Did the hairstyle recommendations make sense?
- Did the preview still look like the same person?
- Did the hairstyle feel useful or realistic?
- Did the hair color feel useful or realistic?
- What part felt confusing?
- What made you hesitate or lose trust?
- What would make you trust the result more?

## Known Beta Limitations

Please keep these in mind:
- The AI preview may slightly change facial details.
- The result is hairstyle direction, not a final salon guarantee.
- There is no login yet.
- There is no payment or top-up yet.
- History is still beta and uses local/backend-memory behavior.
